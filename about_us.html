Marina Fleet

Discover the beautiful city from a unique perspective with one of the most experienced companies. Marina Fleet has been showcasing the city and its important river landmarks to locals and visitors alike over years. Our fleet is used for Sightseeing, Functions and Private Events. Each fleet has large windows and an outside deck area for viewing the River and its surrounding scenery, as well as on-board amenities and staff to assist passengers along their river cruise

With a growing fleet of private yachts and boats available for charter, we can accommodate virtually any party or event from 1 to 1,000 guests for something as simple as an intimate dinner cruise for a romantic couple to large corporate events. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing tour, a wedding at sea, a sport fishing excursion, a sunset cruise, a “boatel” for a unique overnight experience, or an exciting day of dolphin and whale watching, we have a private yacht available to accommodate you and your guests
